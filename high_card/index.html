Rules:

  1. Game is first to 5 or whoever has the most points once all 20 cards are played (10 from both player and CPU).
  2. Each round is played where the player and CPU select one card (CPU card is random) and the person with the highest value wins the round.
  3. Both players can only play a card one time per complete game. Once a round is over, the card will be removed from the player's respective hands.
  4. Once you play your card, whoever has the higher value will get a point above the player box, and to go to the next round, click on the "Start Next Round" Button.
  5. If you want to reset to play a fresh game, hit the "Reset Game" button.
